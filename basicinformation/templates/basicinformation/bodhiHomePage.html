BodhiAI

a personal assistant for every teacher and student



Bodhi AI is providing revolutionary education system with help of modern technology Artificial Intelligence. Traditionally schools adapt one-size-fits-all approach to teaching. But students learn at different pace and have different progress rates. Teachers find it difficult to identify and deal with educational needs of large number of students. Bodhi AI helps teachers to find gaps in their teaching and point to where students are struggling with subject matter.
